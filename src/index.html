NISHABDHA (निशब्द) — derived from Sanskrit, meaning "silence" or "without sound."

We are a creative studio that transforms the unspoken into powerful visual narratives. In the space between noise and stillness, we find stories waiting to be told.

Our work speaks in frames, colors, and motion — giving voice to what words cannot express.

"In silence, we create. In creation, we speak."

PORTFOLIO

Inside Our Creative Studio

Podcast Room

A professionally tuned environment built for long form conversations and broadcast-grade audio. Featuring acoustic treatment panels, studio monitors, multi channel mixing capability, and a multi camera setup. Every session is captured with cinematic clarity and pristine sound.

Green Screen Studio

A dedicated chroma key environment engineered for seamless composing, VFX shoots, and virtual production. Floor to wall green coverage paired with professional Godox lighting rigs ensures uniform key ready illumination letting your creative vision define the world behind the frame.

Photoshoot Area

A versatile photography environment built for product, portrait, editorial and fashion shoots. Equipped with professional strobe systems, continuous LED panels, and a range of backdrop configurations adaptable to any creative brief with precision and elegance.

Makeup Room

A dedicated preparation space designed for artists, models and on screen talent. Featuring daylight balanced Hollywood mirrors, professional vanity stations, and a calm, focused atmosphere. every detail considered so your team arrives on set camera ready and confident.

Dubbing &
Voice Recording Room

A sonically isolated recording environment purpose built for voiceover work, dubbing, ADR, and narration. Treated walls eliminate reflections and external noise intrusion, ensuring studio grade recording that meet broadcast and streaing delivery standards.
